At The Museum

Accessability

Cleaning and Safety
We have instituted many measures that abide by the scientific evidence for best health standards including enhanced cleaning protocols, one-way directional flow where appropriate, and reduced occupancy within the Museum to allow for safe distancing.
Assistive listening devices, scooters and wheelchairs are still available and subject to enhanced cleaning procedures

Special Facilities
Our museum is up to top-modern standard, meaning it’s availalable for you no matter your needs. We have full wheelchair access on most of our exhibits, all restrooms, shop and cafè.
Please get in touch if you feel the need to plan you stay in regards to accesability, we are happy to help!

Read more about our acceessability here
Or ask us any questions you may have at facilities@csm.com

PASSENGER

CAFE AND RESTAURANT


We are so proud and happy to have eco-friendly and local star Passenger as our food provider for our curious and hungry scientist.

Passenger only uses local produce, while still keeping prices down, making this a option for any visiting family or group. At daytime they can offer healthy small snacks to bring on your tour, and of course some caffeein for the adult in charge. For lunch or dinner there’s a wide variety of filling meals, smaller/children friendly meals, vegetarian and vegan options, and not to mention: baked goods and something sweet, made in the café istself!

We believe there is something for every taste. Our big dining area is easily changed into to bigger or smaller tables fitting to your family or group. Visit our restaurant before your start your tour, and book your meal and reserved seats for when you finish!

Visit passenger.com to find out more about what they offer.

SHOP

Continue the learning also after retruning home, or maybe get a picture of the whole family?

Our shop is open during openingshours, offering anything from toys to scientific experiements. Grow your own T-Rex at home from just water, or train your brain with problem solving toys!
We believe that learning is best done by hand and your own accomplement, and this shows in our inventory. Our wonderful and experienced shop assistants will help you find just what your looking for.

We also have a photo boot for you to receive a memory of your day at the museum, at a affordable price.
