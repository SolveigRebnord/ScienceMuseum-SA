Become a member of our Holiday Club!

For every holiday, there’s something new to explore!

Christmas, Easter, Halloween, Summer Break or maybe just a made up one. We want to invite you for a gathering to celebrate as often as possible, for you to meet new and old friends while learning something new.

There will be food, there will be all the tools you need to make something spectacular, and there will be friendly staff to take care of both you (and your parents) All you have to do, is bring your curiousity and excitement turned to the max, and together we will make each holiday better than the last one!

DONT WORRY PARENTS! There’s an adult membership for you as well. Same conditions as for the original, only a little less VIP

Included in membership:

*VIP passage and reserved spot every holiday event
(at least four times a year)

*Filling lunch/dinner pluss drinks + snacks

*Everything included no matter the activity or workshop, and of course you get to bring home everything you make!

Become a member of our Young Star Club!

For every sunday, there’s something new to explore!

We want to combine a group of young, aspring to-be-stars for sundays filled with friends and learning of the highest tier. Each sunday for 8 weeks we will have a new subject, or a new field to explore.

For us, it is important to have fun and engange, but our goal is also to teach our visitors how to use science and wisdom to do good. Sustainability is crucial, and we want to teach them the mindset of implementing their knowledge to do good choices. No matter if they are going to space, finding the cure for diseases or building a time machine.

Our activity is aimed to be for children only, but parents are of course welcome to stay at the museum, have a coffee in the café or engage if needed.

Included in membership:

* A workshop/activity every sunday 10am - 14pm for 8 weeks in total

* Filling lunch/dinner pluss drinks + snacks

* Everything included no matter the activity or workshop, and of course you get to bring home everything you make!

Education

As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring. Our team are on hand to give your students guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning laboratory, and provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them.

Please get in touch with us to find out more about our facilities and to arrange a time to bring your students through for a visit
